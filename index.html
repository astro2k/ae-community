Information

Aesthetic Clan, or more widely known as AE Clan, has been around for almost a year! The clan, founded in June 2nd is still striving to this day! In the past year, it has been through its ups and downs. Here are some defining moments in Aesthetics history that helped shape it into what it is today. Aesthetic today is a communtiy for everyone!

Creation

A server of no less than 50 people. In fact, we weren't even known as AE until the 50 member milestone, we were known as a different name, something for you to find out ;). The staff team were Leaders and Co-Leaders only. It consisted of 2 Leaders and 3 Co-Leaders. Rumination and Leo were the Leaders while Dim, Cignus, and Uber took up the Co-Leader positions.

Motivation

The server was at its most best form when our staff had proper motivation towards the server. When everyone gave their best the clan / server started averaging between 1-5k messages per day. when we were finally settled we averaged 17-k a day with maximum 20 guest joins daily. We have come a long way and our motivation is each other, We just give our best and positivness happens!

Our History


The clan started out by playing 'Balz.io', a very popular io game at that period. In that period, the server was very active for a server of its size reaching sometimes 3-4k messages daily and 1+ members joining a day. With all of this influx of new members, Moderators were needed. The first appointed Moderators were Wizard, Magic, and Proxx. They carried out tasks like keeping chats clean and all of the normal business a Moderator would do. After setting a solid staff team for the clan, we talked about changing our clan name to something more unique. We realised that the competition was very large, with other big clans dominating Balz.io. Finally, on June the 6th... we hit 50 members!!
As we first entered Balz, we were a very small clan getting pushed around by bigger clans. We tried our best to recruit players, and slowly build up our foundation to match other clans. The first montage was released for the clan at June 18th, 16 days after the creation of the clan. We initiated our first takeover at June the 7th, overall it was a huge success. In addition, the montage was made by our first editor, Kento. Shortly after the montage, we began attracting new members, and slowly we got bigger and bigger. We officially hit the BIG 100 at June the 14th. Only 12 days after the creation of the clan.
